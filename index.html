I am a PhD candidate in economics at Northwestern University. My research interests include macroeconomics, computational methods, and time-series econometrics. Please see my research page for more info on my projects or my CV for a summary of my work and background.

My teaching page contains materials I've put together for my students, including notes, slides, and code. It's important to me that everything is both clear and thorough, so feedback is always welcome.

Every year, during the NCAA Basketball Championships, I do some simple modelling in order to fill out my brackets. Recent forecasts can be found on my March Madness page.

I can be reached via email at majic@u.northwestern.edu. My programs, if not posted on this site, may appear on my GitHub.
